{{ if game_number == 1 }}

In this part, you will play a card game with your coparticipant in 3 rounds. The person who can win at least 2 rounds of these 3 rounds will get 90 tokens and the loser will get 30 tokens.

At the start of each card game, you will be dealt some cards and your coparticipant will also be dealt some cards. Each card has a number which can be any of the whole numbers in the range from 1 to 10. Each of these numbers is equally likely at each round. Dealing a deck of cards in each round is mutual for you and your coparticipant which means you and your coparticipant do not have the same card numbers. For example, if your coparticipant has card number 5 and card number 8, your cards will not be any of these two cards. The chance of having a card is the same for you and your coparticipant. In each round, the holder of the higher number card among you and your coparticipant is the winner of the round. The person who can wins at least 2 rounds of 3 rounds is the overall winner.

In each round of the card game, you and your coparticipant will have several cards. Your task is to choose one card from your cards. The number of cards you can have depends on your group. In the previous task, you are assigned to {{ participant.group }} group and your coparticipant is selected randomly from another group. In this session for task 3, all participants who are in {{ participant.group }} group have only 2 cards, but other participants who are in another group have 3 cards. So, you have only 2 cards in each round, and you should choose your card from these two cards. While your coparticipant has 3 cards.

While you are making your decisions about choosing your card, you will not know what decisions your coparticipant is making. Nor will you know the numbers on the cards that are dealt to him or her. Similarly, your coparticipant will not know what decisions you are making, or the numbers on the cards that are dealt to you.

After you and your coparticipant have made your decision, you can observe the selected cards by you and your coparticipant in the next page. Whoever has the card with the higher number wins that round of the game. The person who can wins at least 2 rounds of 3 rounds is the overall winner. The overall winner will get 90 token and the overall loser will get 30 tokens.

Please raise your hand if you have any questions. Before you start to take decisions, we ask you to answer some questions in the next several screens. The purpose of these questions is to check whether you have under-stood these instructions. Any mistake you may make in doing these questions will not affect your final money earnings.

When you have finished Task3 part 1, please remain seated. When everyone has finished this part, you will see the instructions for task 3 part 2.
